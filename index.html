Tracks and Strands

HUMSS

The HUMSS Strand is for students who intend to pursue studies in the fields of Humanities and Social Sciences at the tertiary level.

ABM

The ABM Strand is for those who plan to take up business-related courses in higher education or engage in business, entrepreneurship, and other business-related careers.

TVL

Information and Communications Technology (ICT) Strand and Agri-Fishery Arts Strand are Technical-Vocational-Livelihood Track, and is for SHS learners who intend to pursue careers specializing in vocational learning or technical-vocational livelihood.
